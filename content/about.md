Shawn Zeng is currently part of the **Digital Analytics Team at Hilton APAC Headquarters** in Shanghai. He specializes in ensuring data integrity and driving strategic growth through tracking data validation (QA), detailed feature analysis, and the development of technical specifications. He is also a key advocate for **AI transformation** within the team, exploring innovative ways to leverage artificial intelligence in analytics.

He is a writer and curator of ideas, focusing on the intersection of technology, culture, and personal growth. He holds an undergraduate degree from the **Marriott Program** at **TUC-FIU** (a joint program between **Tianjin University of Commerce** and **Florida International University**). He also graduated from **East China Normal University** with a degree in **Library and Information Science**, a background that fuels his passion for organizing information and uncovering hidden insights.

He continues to share his insights on his personal blog and newsletter. You can find him exploring the latest in digital trends and personal development.
